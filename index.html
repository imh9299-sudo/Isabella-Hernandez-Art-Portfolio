About

Isabella Hernandez is a visual artist from Guatemala who combines digital and traditional techniques to explore the intersection of cultural heritage and fantasy. Her work draws inspiration from pre-Columbian civilizations and her Guatemalan roots, while also incorporating original characters, creatures, and settings developed for a fantasy novel in progress. Through her art, she seeks to honor ancestral traditions while imagining new, speculative worlds.
